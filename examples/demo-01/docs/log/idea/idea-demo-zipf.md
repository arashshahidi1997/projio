# Investigate Zipf's law in text corpus

Word frequency distributions in natural language follow Zipf's law:
the frequency of a word is inversely proportional to its rank.

## Success criteria

- Plot word rank vs frequency on log-log axes
- Confirm approximate linear relationship (slope ≈ -1)
