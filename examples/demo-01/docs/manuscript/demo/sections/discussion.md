# Discussion

Our findings confirm that Zipf's law holds in the demo corpus, consistent with
prior observations [@zipf1949]. The robustness of this pattern across even
small text samples suggests that the underlying mechanism operates at a
fundamental level of language production.

Future work should extend this analysis to larger corpora and explore the
relationship between text genre and the power-law exponent.
